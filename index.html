Overview

Current version: 1.0.0  •  Platform: iOS

Prepare for the UK Car Theory Test with topic practice, realistic mock tests, clear explanations, and progress tracking. Change the app language or use dual-language mode to study with two languages side by side.

English (EN) Turkish (TR) Albanian (SQ) Polish (PL) Romanian (RO) Dual: EN‑TR Dual: EN‑SQ Dual: EN‑PL Dual: EN‑RO

Quick Help

  • Find Dual-Language Mode: Language → choose a language (e.g. TR,PL,SQ).
  • Topic Practice: Pick a topic from the syllabus to target weak areas.
  • Mock Tests: Start a mock from the home screen; timed and scored like the real exam.
  • Progress: See stats by topic; use this to plan revision.
  • No Hazard Videos: This app focuses on multiple-choice car theory only.

Troubleshooting

  • Mock test won’t start: Ensure stable internet, free storage, then restart the app. Reinstall if needed.
  • Progress not updating: Restart the app.
  • Language not changing: Restart the app after switching languages.
  • Crashes or freezes: Reboot device, update iOS and the app to the latest version.
